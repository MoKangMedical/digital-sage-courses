这节课单独拆 平台。对 马克·扎克伯格 来说，平台 不是一个口号，而是决定资源如何流动、判断如何排序、风险如何暴露的关键变量。

常见误区Common misreads

  • 把 平台 当成永远正确的答案，而不是一种带条件的判断工具。Confusing a memorable quote with the real decision process behind it.
  • 只会在顺风局谈 平台，一到高压环境就退回短期直觉。Copying the style of the thinker without checking the constraints of your own context.
  • 把 平台 简化成风格偏好，没有落实到决策顺序和指标观察上。Using a single principle too rigidly instead of letting the system of principles interact.

Prioritize technical talks, launches, memos, and long interviews to verify how platform bets, distribution, and engineering constraints were described.

路线迁移节点 Architecture shifts

Focus on model shifts, platform migrations, and open-versus-closed decisions, because methods become clearest when the track changes.
