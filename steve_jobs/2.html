Identify the most important conceptual variable

关键追问 Key opening question 这件东西最核心的体验是什么？ What is the simplest end-to-end experience worth obsessing over, and what should be removed to protect it?

这是 史蒂夫·乔布斯 在复杂问题前会先回到的起点。

This is the question Steve Jobs would return to before rushing into action.

底层支柱 Core pillars 产品 / 审美 / 端到端体验 product taste / aesthetic judgment / end-to-end experience

课程内容始终围绕这三根支柱组织，而不是零散知识点。

The lesson is organized around these three pillars rather than isolated quotations.

本课解决什么问题What this lesson solves

这节课单独拆 产品。对 史蒂夫·乔布斯 来说，产品 不是一个口号，而是决定资源如何流动、判断如何排序、风险如何暴露的关键变量。

With Jobs, product taste is never decoration. It is a decision about what deserves attention and what must disappear. This lesson is about learning when product taste deserves to lead and when it has to be balanced by aesthetic judgment and end-to-end experience.

史蒂夫·乔布斯思想的核心概念：产品。产品是史蒂夫·乔布斯思想体系的基石。先抓住 产品 的第一关键变量，再讨论表达方式和执行顺序。理解这个概念，是进入史蒂夫·乔布斯世界的第一步。本课将深入剖析产品的深层逻辑与实践含义。

This stage is about one pillar at a time. The goal is not definition-memorization but better diagnostic use of product taste.

概念定义 What this concept really does

在 史蒂夫·乔布斯 的语境里，产品 关注的是“先看什么、再做什么”，而不是漂亮表达。

With Jobs, product taste is never decoration. It is a decision about what deserves attention and what must disappear.

与其他支柱的关系 What it must be paired with

产品 必须和 审美、端到端体验 一起看，否则很容易变成片面执念。

Read product taste together with aesthetic judgment and end-to-end experience, or it turns into a slogan.

边界条件 Where readers usually slip

当 产品 看起来正确但结果不对时，通常说明约束不在概念本身，而在场景判断或执行节奏。

The mistake is treating product taste as a universal virtue instead of a contextual judgment tool.

判断清单Judgment checklist

  • 如果去掉 产品，史蒂夫·乔布斯 的整套方法会先失去哪一块判断力。Ask what breaks first if product taste is ignored in a live decision.
  • 在你的领域里，产品 对应的真实观测指标是什么，而不只是情绪上的“感觉”。Test whether your current use of product taste is structural or merely rhetorical.
  • 产品 和 审美 出现冲突时，应该先看结构性约束还是短期表现。Check what aesthetic judgment or end-to-end experience would add before you become one-dimensional.
  • 什么时候必须坚持 产品，什么时候要承认它只是局部最优。Translate product taste into one observable indicator in your own context.

常见误区Common misreads

  • 把 产品 当成永远正确的答案，而不是一种带条件的判断工具。Treating product taste as a permanent answer rather than a conditional lens.
  • 只会在顺风局谈 产品，一到高压环境就退回短期直觉。Using product taste in easy situations but abandoning it under pressure.
  • 把 产品 简化成风格偏好，没有落实到决策顺序和指标观察上。Talking about product taste elegantly without changing decision order or measurement.
