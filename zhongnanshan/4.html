本课解决什么问题What this lesson solves

这节课单独拆 实话实说。对 钟南山 来说，实话实说 不是一个口号，而是决定资源如何流动、判断如何排序、风险如何暴露的关键变量。

This lesson uses Zhong Nanshan to train a repeatable way of seeing 循证, 公共卫生, and 实话实说 as one system. The point is not to imitate the tone, but to learn the order of judgment behind the voice.

深入钟南山思想的第三根支柱：实话实说。实话实说与循证、公共卫生构成三角支撑。钟南山说：以人为本：医学不仅是指标，更是病人的生活质量和选择。本课通过案例展示实话实说的实践力量。

This lesson belongs to the Core Concept III stage of the curriculum and should end in a visible operating takeaway.

概念定义 Core lens

在 钟南山 的语境里，实话实说 关注的是“先看什么、再做什么”，而不是漂亮表达。

Read the issue through 循证 before chasing noise or surface-level activity.

常见误区Common misreads

  • 把 实话实说 当成永远正确的答案，而不是一种带条件的判断工具。Confusing a memorable quote with the real decision process behind it.
  • 只会在顺风局谈 实话实说，一到高压环境就退回短期直觉。Copying the style of the thinker without checking the constraints of your own context.
  • 把 实话实说 简化成风格偏好，没有落实到决策顺序和指标观察上。Using a single principle too rigidly instead of letting the system of principles interact.
临床与公共卫生原始材料 Clinical record

优先看指南、临床论文、公开通报、病例讨论和权威访谈，验证风险排序和证据口径。

Start with guidelines, clinical papers, public briefings, case discussions, and authoritative interviews to verify risk ranking and evidence standards.

危机与救治节点 Crisis decisions

重点回看疫情、救治、筛查和沟通节点，因为方法论在高压环境下最容易显形。

Focus on outbreak, treatment, screening, and communication decisions, because methods are most visible under pressure.

医学史与人物回顾 Medical history

最后再用人物回顾、医学史和机构档案来补充长期影响。

Then use retrospective profiles, institutional archives, and medical history to understand longer-term impact.
