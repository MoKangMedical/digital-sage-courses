适合带着什么问题来学 What problem should you bring into the course?

优先带着这类问题进入：当信息不全但必须快速决策时，先排哪类风险。

Start with a live problem such as this: 当信息不全但必须快速决策时，先排哪类风险。

这一套课真正训练什么 What does this curriculum actually train?

训练你把 最需要优先排除的危险是什么？ 变成稳定的判断起点。

Zhong Nanshan is best read through evidence discipline, public responsibility, and clinical realism under pressure.

学完之后能迁移到哪里 Where does it transfer?

最终不是模仿 钟南山，而是把 循证, 公共卫生, 实话实说 迁移到你自己的业务和选择。

The real lesson is not reputation. It is learning how evidence-based judgment behaves under public pressure.

建议接着对读谁 Who should you compare next?

同领域建议接着对读：伊丽莎白·布莱克本、保罗·法默、大卫·辛克莱。这样能看见同一问题在不同路径下的取舍差异。

After this course, compare with Elizabeth Blackburn, Paul Farmer, David Sinclair. That contrast makes trade-offs inside the same domain easier to see.

Curriculum Arc

钟南山 的 10 课，不是知识点列表，而是一条递进学习弧线。The 10 lessons for Zhong Nanshan form a progression, not a list of quotes.

前四课建立图谱与三根支柱，第 5 到 7 课把判断框架和工具箱拆开，第 8 到 10 课回到价值系统、可执行方法和个人行动整合。

The first four lessons build the map and three pillars. Lessons five to seven open up judgment order and tools. The final three return to values, operating method, and integration.

Reference Shelf

钟南山 的原典与书单Trusted texts for Zhong Nanshan

钟南山更适合通过论文、指南、公开通报和长访谈来读，而不是找一本包打天下的“人物书”。他的价值主要在于证据、临床和公共沟通三者如何连成一体。

Zhong Nanshan is best read through papers, guidelines, public briefings, and long interviews rather than a single definitive book. His importance lies in how evidence, clinic, and public communication get tied together.
