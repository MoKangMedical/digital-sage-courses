The lesson is organized around these three pillars rather than isolated quotations.

本课解决什么问题What this lesson solves

这节课单独拆 反诘。对 苏格拉底 来说，反诘 不是一个口号，而是决定资源如何流动、判断如何排序、风险如何暴露的关键变量。

For Socrates, questioning matters because it reveals whether the current definition can survive examination. This lesson is about learning when questioning deserves to lead and when it has to be balanced by virtue and self-examination.

苏格拉底思想的核心概念：反诘。反诘是苏格拉底思想体系的基石。先抓住 反诘 的第一关键变量，再讨论表达方式和执行顺序。理解这个概念，是进入苏格拉底世界的第一步。本课将深入剖析反诘的深层逻辑与实践含义。

This stage is about one pillar at a time. The goal is not definition-memorization but better diagnostic use of questioning.

概念定义 What this concept really does

在 苏格拉底 的语境里，反诘 关注的是“先看什么、再做什么”，而不是漂亮表达。

For Socrates, questioning matters because it reveals whether the current definition can survive examination.

与其他支柱的关系 What it must be paired with

反诘 必须和 德性、认识自己 一起看，否则很容易变成片面执念。

Read questioning together with virtue and self-examination, or it turns into a slogan.

边界条件 Where readers usually slip

当 反诘 看起来正确但结果不对时，通常说明约束不在概念本身，而在场景判断或执行节奏。

The mistake is treating questioning as a universal virtue instead of a contextual judgment tool.

判断清单Judgment checklist

  • 如果去掉 反诘，苏格拉底 的整套方法会先失去哪一块判断力。Ask what breaks first if questioning is ignored in a live decision.
  • 在你的领域里，反诘 对应的真实观测指标是什么，而不只是情绪上的“感觉”。Test whether your current use of questioning is structural or merely rhetorical.
  • 反诘 和 德性 出现冲突时，应该先看结构性约束还是短期表现。Check what virtue or self-examination would add before you become one-dimensional.
  • 什么时候必须坚持 反诘，什么时候要承认它只是局部最优。Translate questioning into one observable indicator in your own context.

常见误区Common misreads

  • 把 反诘 当成永远正确的答案，而不是一种带条件的判断工具。Treating questioning as a permanent answer rather than a conditional lens.
  • 只会在顺风局谈 反诘，一到高压环境就退回短期直觉。Using questioning in easy situations but abandoning it under pressure.
  • 把 反诘 简化成风格偏好，没有落实到决策顺序和指标观察上。Talking about questioning elegantly without changing decision order or measurement.
