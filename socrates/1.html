本课解决什么问题What this lesson solves

这节课先不急着记定义，而是先把 苏格拉底 这套方法论的整体骨架搭起来。你会看到 反诘、德性、认识自己 如何彼此配合，以及 我们在讨论的概念到底指什么？ 为什么会成为 苏格拉底 反复回到的起点。

This opening lesson maps Socrates as a complete decision system. Socrates is best read as a discipline of questioning, moral seriousness, and intellectual humility under pressure. The task is to see how questioning, virtue, and self-examination reinforce one another before you start borrowing isolated moves.

常见误区Common misreads

  • 把 苏格拉底 误解成只会输出结论，而忽略他如何定义问题。Reducing Socrates to temperament or style instead of structure.
  • 只盯住 反诘 的表面形式，却没有看它为什么在这个领域成立。Treating questioning, virtue, and self-examination as separate tricks rather than a coordinated system.
  • 把 苏格拉底 的成功归因于天赋，而不是长期重复的判断纪律。Borrowing conclusions without learning what gets examined first.
