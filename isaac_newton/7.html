本课解决什么问题What this lesson solves

这节课要把 艾萨克·牛顿 的经验压缩成可反复调用的工具箱。工具箱的价值在于：当场景变化时，你不用重新发明方法，只需要判断该调用哪一个模型。

This lesson uses Isaac Newton to train a repeatable way of seeing 定律, 演绎, and 专注 as one system. The point is not to imitate the tone, but to learn the order of judgment behind the voice.

艾萨克·牛顿的思维模型工具箱。第一，逆向思维：如果我看得更远，是因为站在巨人的肩膀上。第二，长期视角：证据优先：先看数据和实验，再谈立场。第三，系统思维：现在有哪些可靠事实？。第四，概率思维：追求有利的不对称性。

This lesson belongs to the Mental Models Toolkit stage of the curriculum and should end in a visible operating takeaway.

Read the issue through 定律 before chasing noise or surface-level activity.

模型三 Learning outcome

用 演绎 和 专注 检查节奏与边界，避免局部最优。

By the end, you should be able to restate the lesson in your own work context.

常见误区Common misreads

  • 收藏很多模型名词，却从未在真实问题中实际调用。Confusing a memorable quote with the real decision process behind it.
  • 把工具箱做成知识清单，而不是决策清单。Copying the style of the thinker without checking the constraints of your own context.
  • 只保留让自己舒服的模型，没有保留提醒自己踩刹车的模型。Using a single principle too rigidly instead of letting the system of principles interact.
