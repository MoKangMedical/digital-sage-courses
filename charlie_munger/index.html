Curriculum Arc

查理·芒格 的 10 课，不是知识点列表，而是一条递进学习弧线。The 10 lessons for Charlie Munger form a progression, not a list of quotes.

前四课建立图谱与三根支柱，第 5 到 7 课把判断框架和工具箱拆开，第 8 到 10 课回到价值系统、可执行方法和个人行动整合。

The first four lessons build the map and three pillars. Lessons five to seven open up judgment order and tools. The final three return to values, operating method, and integration.

Reference Shelf

查理·芒格 的原典与书单Trusted texts for Charlie Munger

芒格要先读演讲和讲话原文，再读整理书和二手阐释。不要先从“思维模型”标签开始，要先看他如何反复讲“少犯错”。

Read the talks first, then the edited collections and biographies. Do not start with the label 'mental models' before seeing how often Munger is really teaching error-avoidance.

原典与公开记录Primary texts and public record

原典 / 一手记录 Primary text / public record Poor Charlie's Almanack Charles T. Munger, edited by Peter D. Kaufman · talks and collected materials

最接近芒格本人表达节奏和结构的一本书。

The closest single-volume entrance to Munger's own cadence and reasoning.

原典 / 一手记录 Primary text / public record A Lesson on Elementary, Worldly Wisdom as It Relates to Investment Management & Business Charlie Munger · speech

多元模型框架的核心演讲。

The essential speech on the latticework of mental models.

原典 / 一手记录 Primary text / public record The Psychology of Human Misjudgment Charlie Munger · speech

读芒格一定要回到这篇，认知偏误部分最集中。

A foundational text if you want the clearest statement of Munger on cognitive error.

核心书单与研究入口Core reading shelf

核心书单 / 研究入口 Core reading / study entry Damn Right! Behind the Scenes with Berkshire Hathaway Billionaire Charlie Munger Janet Lowe · biography

补足芒格的生平、职业脉络与投资实践。

Useful for life history, career context, and investment practice.

核心书单 / 研究入口 Core reading / study entry Seeking Wisdom: From Darwin to Munger Peter Bevelin · interpretive book

不是芒格原著，但很适合把他的跨学科框架转成可用清单。

Not authored by Munger, but strong at turning his framework into usable judgment tools.

核心书单 / 研究入口 Core reading / study entry Charlie Munger: The Complete Investor Tren Griffin · interpretive book

适合快速梳理芒格的投资原则与心理学框架。

A concise map of Munger's investment principles and psychology-heavy worldview.
