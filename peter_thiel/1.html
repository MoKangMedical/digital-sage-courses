本课解决什么问题What this lesson solves

这节课先不急着记定义，而是先把 彼得·蒂尔 这套方法论的整体骨架搭起来。你会看到 垄断、反共识、零到一 如何彼此配合，以及 这件事真正创造价值的环节是什么？ 为什么会成为 彼得·蒂尔 反复回到的起点。

This opening lesson maps Peter Thiel as a complete decision system. Thiel is best read as a strategist of contrarian truth, technological leverage, and asymmetric company building. The task is to see how monopoly, contrarian truth, and zero-to-one thinking reinforce one another before you start borrowing isolated moves.

常见误区Common misreads

  • 把 彼得·蒂尔 误解成只会输出结论，而忽略他如何定义问题。Reducing Peter Thiel to temperament or style instead of structure.
  • 只盯住 垄断 的表面形式，却没有看它为什么在这个领域成立。Treating monopoly, contrarian truth, and zero-to-one thinking as separate tricks rather than a coordinated system.
