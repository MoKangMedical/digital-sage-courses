课程内容始终围绕这三根支柱组织，而不是零散知识点。

The lesson is organized around these three pillars rather than isolated quotations.

本课解决什么问题What this lesson solves

这节课单独拆 形式化。对 艾伦·图灵 来说，形式化 不是一个口号，而是决定资源如何流动、判断如何排序、风险如何暴露的关键变量。

With Turing, formalization becomes a test of whether the problem can be represented rigorously enough to be worked on at all. This lesson is about learning when formalization deserves to lead and when it has to be balanced by computation and machine intelligence.

艾伦·图灵思想的第二个核心概念：形式化。形式化与计算形成互补关系。如果说计算解决看什么的问题，形式化解决怎么做的问题。本课深入分析形式化的深层含义。

This stage is about one pillar at a time. The goal is not definition-memorization but better diagnostic use of formalization.

概念定义 What this concept really does

在 艾伦·图灵 的语境里，形式化 关注的是“先看什么、再做什么”，而不是漂亮表达。

With Turing, formalization becomes a test of whether the problem can be represented rigorously enough to be worked on at all.

与其他支柱的关系 What it must be paired with

形式化 必须和 计算、智能 一起看，否则很容易变成片面执念。

Read formalization together with computation and machine intelligence, or it turns into a slogan.

边界条件 Where readers usually slip

当 形式化 看起来正确但结果不对时，通常说明约束不在概念本身，而在场景判断或执行节奏。

The mistake is treating formalization as a universal virtue instead of a contextual judgment tool.

常见误区Common misreads

  • 把 形式化 当成永远正确的答案，而不是一种带条件的判断工具。Treating formalization as a permanent answer rather than a conditional lens.
  • 只会在顺风局谈 形式化，一到高压环境就退回短期直觉。Using formalization in easy situations but abandoning it under pressure.
  • 把 形式化 简化成风格偏好，没有落实到决策顺序和指标观察上。Talking about formalization elegantly without changing decision order or measurement.
