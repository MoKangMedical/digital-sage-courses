本课解决什么问题What this lesson solves

边界条件 Where readers usually slip

当 同理心 看起来正确但结果不对时，通常说明约束不在概念本身，而在场景判断或执行节奏。

The mistake is treating empathy as a universal virtue instead of a contextual judgment tool.

常见误区Common misreads

  • 把 同理心 当成永远正确的答案，而不是一种带条件的判断工具。Treating empathy as a permanent answer rather than a conditional lens.
  • 只会在顺风局谈 同理心，一到高压环境就退回短期直觉。Using empathy in easy situations but abandoning it under pressure.
  • 把 同理心 简化成风格偏好，没有落实到决策顺序和指标观察上。Talking about empathy elegantly without changing decision order or measurement.
