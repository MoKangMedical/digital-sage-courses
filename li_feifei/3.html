本课解决什么问题What this lesson solves

这节课单独拆 视觉智能。对 李飞飞 来说，视觉智能 不是一个口号，而是决定资源如何流动、判断如何排序、风险如何暴露的关键变量。

For Fei-Fei Li, vision intelligence matters when it deepens intelligence while keeping the human context visible rather than abstracted away. This lesson is about learning when vision intelligence deserves to lead and when it has to be balanced by human-centered AI and education.

李飞飞思想的第二个核心概念：视觉智能。视觉智能与人本 AI形成互补关系。如果说人本 AI解决看什么的问题，视觉智能解决怎么做的问题。本课深入分析视觉智能的深层含义。

This stage is about one pillar at a time. The goal is not definition-memorization but better diagnostic use of vision intelligence.

与其他支柱的关系 What it must be paired with

视觉智能 必须和 人本 AI、教育 一起看，否则很容易变成片面执念。

Read vision intelligence together with human-centered AI and education, or it turns into a slogan.

边界条件 Where readers usually slip

当 视觉智能 看起来正确但结果不对时，通常说明约束不在概念本身，而在场景判断或执行节奏。

The mistake is treating vision intelligence as a universal virtue instead of a contextual judgment tool.

常见误区Common misreads

  • 把 视觉智能 当成永远正确的答案，而不是一种带条件的判断工具。Treating vision intelligence as a permanent answer rather than a conditional lens.
  • 只会在顺风局谈 视觉智能，一到高压环境就退回短期直觉。Using vision intelligence in easy situations but abandoning it under pressure.
