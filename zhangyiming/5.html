本课解决什么问题What this lesson solves

如果前四课解决的是“看什么”，这节课解决的是“怎么判断”。张一鸣 的强大之处，不在于他总有答案，而在于他有一套稳定的排序顺序。

This lesson uses Zhang Yiming to train a repeatable way of seeing 延迟满足, 算法, and 全球化 as one system. The point is not to imitate the tone, but to learn the order of judgment behind the voice.

张一鸣的四步判断框架。第一步：这件事真正创造价值的环节是什么？。第二步：它的约束是市场、供给，还是组织能力？。第三步：如果把 延迟满足 作为核心变量，现在最容易被忽视的约束是什么？。第四步：围绕 算法 应该做减法还是加法？。这个框架可以在任何复杂决策中反复使用。

This lesson belongs to the Judgment Framework stage of the curriculum and should end in a visible operating takeaway.

第一步 Core lens

先定义问题：这件事真正创造价值的环节是什么？。

Read the issue through 延迟满足 before chasing noise or surface-level activity.

第二步 Question to start with

再确认真正约束，是 延迟满足、算法 还是 全球化 没有到位。

Begin with: What should be examined first when this problem becomes complex?

第三步 Learning outcome

最后才决定资源、节奏和动作，不让执行先于判断。

By the end, you should be able to restate the lesson in your own work context.

判断清单Judgment checklist

  • 任何复杂判断先回到问题定义，而不是直接讨论方案优劣。Track 延迟满足 as a structural variable instead of a slogan.
  • 如果争论越来越大，通常不是意见不同，而是每个人盯的主变量不同。Use 算法 to inspect compounding, rhythm, or second-order effects.

常见误区Common misreads

  • 以为判断框架只是写在纸上的流程，实际没有配套信息筛选规则。Confusing a memorable quote with the real decision process behind it.
  • 一上来讨论解决方案，跳过了问题定义和约束识别。Copying the style of the thinker without checking the constraints of your own context.
  • 把判断框架当成开会语言，没有落实到个人决策节奏。Using a single principle too rigidly instead of letting the system of principles interact.

Start with shareholder letters, annual letters, investor notes, launch memos, and long-form founder interviews to verify how capital allocation and trade-offs were framed over time.
