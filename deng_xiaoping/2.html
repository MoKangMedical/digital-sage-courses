常见误区Common misreads

  • 把 实事求是 当成永远正确的答案，而不是一种带条件的判断工具。Confusing a memorable quote with the real decision process behind it.
  • 只会在顺风局谈 实事求是，一到高压环境就退回短期直觉。Copying the style of the thinker without checking the constraints of your own context.
  • 把 实事求是 简化成风格偏好，没有落实到决策顺序和指标观察上。Using a single principle too rigidly instead of letting the system of principles interact.
