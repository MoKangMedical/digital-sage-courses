For Altman, distribution is only meaningful when it compounds through users, infrastructure, and distribution at the same time. This lesson is about learning when distribution deserves to lead and when it has to be balanced by AGI and startups.

边界条件 Where readers usually slip

当 分发 看起来正确但结果不对时，通常说明约束不在概念本身，而在场景判断或执行节奏。

常见误区Common misreads

  • 把 分发 当成永远正确的答案，而不是一种带条件的判断工具。Treating distribution as a permanent answer rather than a conditional lens.
