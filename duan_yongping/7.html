本课解决什么问题What this lesson solves

这节课要把 段永平 的经验压缩成可反复调用的工具箱。工具箱的价值在于：当场景变化时，你不用重新发明方法，只需要判断该调用哪一个模型。

This lesson uses Duan Yongping to train a repeatable way of seeing 本分, 长期, and 企业文化 as one system. The point is not to imitate the tone, but to learn the order of judgment behind the voice.

段永平的思维模型工具箱。第一，逆向思维：做对的事，把事做对。第二，长期视角：长期主义：把十年尺度放在一年情绪之前。第三，系统思维：这件事真正创造价值的环节是什么？。第四，概率思维：追求有利的不对称性。

This lesson belongs to the Mental Models Toolkit stage of the curriculum and should end in a visible operating takeaway.

常见误区Common misreads

  • 收藏很多模型名词，却从未在真实问题中实际调用。Confusing a memorable quote with the real decision process behind it.
  • 把工具箱做成知识清单，而不是决策清单。Copying the style of the thinker without checking the constraints of your own context.
  • 只保留让自己舒服的模型，没有保留提醒自己踩刹车的模型。Using a single principle too rigidly instead of letting the system of principles interact.

Start with shareholder letters, annual letters, investor notes, launch memos, and long-form founder interviews to verify how capital allocation and trade-offs were framed over time.
